About DevGenie!

The Idea behind DevGenie is to provide a platform for the Developer community where we can come together and share our ideas and experiences. This application was made by coding boot camp students who needed it to be simpler to discover the solutions to your coding questions or posting your thoughts and encounters. Becoming a Developer can be challenging which is why the DevGenie team created this platform so we can learn together. We hope you will enjoy this application and learn and teach your fellow developers.
